Volunteer with Us

Looking for a meaningful way to give back? Volunteering at Bern Community Center offers a rewarding experience where you can make a direct impact on the lives of local residents. We offer a variety of roles including:

  • Event Assistance: Help organize and manage community events, workshops, and celebrations.
  • Tutoring and Mentorship: Support children, teens, and adults through educational programs.
  • Food Bank Support: Assist in organizing and distributing food to those in need.
  • Senior Programs: Spend time with seniors through our companionship and activity programs.
